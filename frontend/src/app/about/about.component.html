What is Spring Marketplace?

Spring Marketplace is an online Full-Stack Produce Market developed by a team of three individuals. The primary goal of this project is to emulate the functionalities found in modern online store applications. The project serves as a comprehensive learning experience, allowing the team to gain proficiency in both front-end and back-end development.


Feel free to Register and check out all the features.

 Using Spring Marketplace as a Buyer
1. Register

2. Start buying on the marketplace

 Using Spring Marketplace as a Seller
1. Register

2. Add your info on the profile page

3. List items directly from your profile

 Buying Produce

1. Browse what's available on the marketplace

2. Click the item you would like to purchase and add the desired quantity to the cart

3. View your cart by clicking the cart icon

4. Click checkout to purchase, or X to remove specific items from the cart
